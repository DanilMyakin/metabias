It feels like everybody is just shilling their ideas, investments, projects, leaving no room for a healthy discussion. Alienating other opinions.
We got tired of biased media. We got tired of BS.
MetaBias is our answer to the problem.
Our goal is to invite different sides to participate in the discussion, while making it clear, who they do represent.
Our goal is to give voice to people, who want to be heard. To give voice to general users and the broad community.
Our goal is to make the process of information consumption easy and quick.
Leveraging the latest developments in NLP to analyze data from the crypto-universe we are going to aggregate all important news, texts, and opinions in one place.
Incorporating different ratings we will enrich our dataset and further enhance the reliability of our models while giving our users voice.
