Everything is biased. People today have to navigate through a forest of different biases. Every bias has people who want to make profits from your decision to believe, to invest, or to retweet.

With Meta///bias you can be more objective, see through the walls of biased opinions.

Meta///bias is your night vision in the world where light is available only for chosen ones. See, what they see from the height of their flight.

Our goal is to invite different sides to participate in the discussion while making it clear, which they do represent.

Our goal is to give voice to people who want to be heard and to give voice to general users and the broad community.

Our goal is to make the process of information consumption easy and quick.

Leveraging the latest Natural Language Processing developments to analyze data from the crypto-universe, we will aggregate all essential news, texts, and opinions in one place.

Incorporating different ratings, we will enrich our dataset and further enhance our models' reliability while giving our users voice.
